Methodology & sources
EU ETS carbon price
Current price €75/t as of April 2026 (S&P Global; Trading Economics). Forecasts: €85–100/t by 2027 (multiple institutional forecasters). Long-run scenario to €300/t based on EY/Enerdata modelling.
Forestry sequestration
5 tCO₂/ha/yr claimable sequestration based on Banc Woodland Woodland Carbon Code project (357 acres, 22,020 tCO₂e over 100 years). Conservative global estimate; Tower Peak's tropical exposure (Latin America) likely implies upside (MIT Climate Portal: up to 11 tCO₂/ha/yr gross).
Carbon credit pricing
Voluntary market average ~$6/t (Ecosystem Marketplace, 2025). Long-run projection $75–125/t by 2035 (EY Net Zero Centre). Model scales linearly between these anchors.
Agriculture sensitivity
Fertiliser = 10% of farm operating costs (European Commission agri-food portal). 4% fertiliser cost rise per €80/t carbon (Bruegel, February 2026 — EU CBAM fertiliser analysis). Baseline EBITDA margin 8.04% (Damodaran, January 2026, Farming/Agriculture sector).
